# Easy Vegan Recipes for Beginners

Welcome to Herbivore Hub! If you're dipping your toes into the world of veganism, starting with easy recipes can make all the difference. These beginner-friendly dishes use simple ingredients and require minimal prep time. Let's explore three quick recipes to get you started.

## 1. Chickpea Salad Bowl

This refreshing salad is packed with protein and ready in under 15 minutes.

**Ingredients:**
- 1 can chickpeas, drained
- 1 cucumber, diced
- 1 tomato, chopped
- Handful of fresh herbs (parsley or mint)
- Olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Rinse chickpeas and add to a bowl.
2. Mix in cucumber, tomato, and herbs.
3. Drizzle with oil and lemon juice; season well.
4. Toss and serve chilled.

Perfect for lunch on the go!

## 2. Avocado Toast with a Twist

Upgrade your breakfast with this creamy, nutrient-dense toast.

**Ingredients:**
- 1 ripe avocado
- 2 slices whole-grain bread
- Cherry tomatoes, halved
- Sprinkle of nutritional yeast
- Fresh basil leaves

**Instructions:**
1. Toast the bread.
2. Mash avocado and spread on toast.
3. Top with tomatoes, nutritional yeast, and basil.
4. Enjoy immediately.

A herbivore's dream start to the day.

## 3. Stir-Fried Veggies and Tofu

A hearty dinner option that's customizable.

**Ingredients:**
- 200g tofu, cubed
- Mixed veggies (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots)
- Garlic and ginger, minced
- Soy sauce and sesame oil
- Rice to serve
